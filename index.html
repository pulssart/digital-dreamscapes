The Vision

Serenity for Your Screen

These wallpapers are designed to bring calm, relaxation, and a sense of serenity to your daily digital experience. Every glance at your screen becomes a moment of peace.

Each image represents hours of meticulous work — from crafting the perfect prompt to hand-retouching every detail. This is a genuine aesthetic pursuit, not a quick generation.

You may notice strange AI artifacts in some pieces. I've deliberately kept them. When AI hallucinates, it tries to understand our world but doesn't quite succeed — so it fills the gaps with what resembles pure imagination and raw creativity.

I love when AI dreams. Mastering this beautiful deviation through prompting and retouching is what makes these visuals truly singular.

Adrien Donot

The Artist Behind the Pixels

HELLO, I'M
PULSSART

By day, I'm a Senior Product Designer crafting digital experiences. But for years, the creative community has known me as Pulssart — a name synonymous with photographic illustrations that blur the line between reality and imagination.

This collection marks my first venture into generative art. My goal? To find a consistent visual language — clean, minimal, yet deeply evocative. Each piece is a meditation on simplicity, where every element serves a purpose and nothing is left to chance.

These wallpapers are the result of countless hours refining prompts, upscaling with precision, and hand-retouching every detail until the vision feels complete.

— Adrien Donot aka Pulssart
